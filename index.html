The Vault: Your Pocket Library

Manage, organize, and view your 3D assets on the go. Whether it's a ZIP file or a raw STL, MeshVault keeps it tidy.

  • Create custom collections and nested folders
  • Preview 3MF and ZIP contents without extracting
  • View 3D models (STL, OBJ, STEP) and G-Code natively
  • Edit metadata and tags for easy searching

MeshVault Hub (PC)

The perfect companion for your desktop slicer workflow. The Hub automatically discovers your phone on the local network.

  • Wireless sync from Phone to PC
  • Mirrors your mobile library structure
  • Direct "Send to Slicer" integration
  • No cables, no cloud uploads, just fast local transfer

Integrated Web Server

Turn your phone into a local file host. Toggle the server on and browse your entire 3D collection from any browser on your network.
